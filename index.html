Support page ChronicleShot Support Welcome to ChronicleShot Support. ChronicleShot helps users reconstruct and review their travel history by analyzing photo metadata in their Apple Photos library and presenting it in a chronology and map format. Contact For support, bug reports, feature requests, or general feedback, please contact: support@your-email.com Please include: • your device model • iOS version • app version • a short description of the issue We aim to respond within 3 to 5 business days. Common Support Topics • Photos not appearing in the app • Locations showing as unknown • Refresh or analysis taking a long time • Questions about chronology grouping • App crashes or display issues Permissions ChronicleShot requires access to your photo library in order to analyze image metadata and build your travel chronology and map. If the app is not working as expected, please check that photo access has been granted in your iPhone Settings. Privacy ChronicleShot is designed to use your photo metadata to generate your chronology and map experience. For more information, please see the Privacy Policy.
